Our Story & Mission

Building a community rooted in faith, love, and service since 1978

Who We Are

Abeka SDA Church was founded in 2005 with a vision to create a welcoming spiritual home. Today we serve over 1,200 members through worship, community outreach, and discipleship programs.

Our Mission

  • To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ
  • To nurture spiritual growth
  • To serve our local community

Our Core Beliefs

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and our final authority.

Salvation

We believe in salvation through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

Community

We believe the Church is God's family for spiritual growth.
